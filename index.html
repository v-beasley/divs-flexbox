Hi, I'm Valerie.

I am a Responsive Designer & Developer who's passionate about creating meaningful visuals with the best user experience. When I'm not working, pursuing side projects or learning new skills, I can be found knitting, tending my garden and exploring on foot.

I was born & raised in Florida and have traveled around the United States as well as in China. Exploring how other people live their daily lives is fascinating to me. I like to travel but really I love to stay awhile in new places and experience all they have to offer.

My Passion: Crafting! Making things with my hands has always been my favorite way to unwind. It's like moving meditation. Anything from painting to knitting and from to baking to pottery. I've made pretty much every gift for my family since I can remember!

Fun Fact: I can wiggle my ears - and not just when I smile! I can even move them one at a time. When I was a kid I thought everyone could and it wasn't until highschool did I learn that I have freakish ear muscle control. I can also 'pop' them whenever I need to.
